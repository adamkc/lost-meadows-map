Lost Meadows of the Sierra Nevada

A random-forest model that predicts meadow locations across the Sierra Nevada and surrounding regions, at the HUC10 watershed scale. Click any watershed to download its predictions or view them on the map.

High vs. medium confidence

High-confidence polygons use a stricter threshold — fewer locations, but each is more likely to be a true meadow. Medium-confidence polygons use a more inclusive threshold: broader coverage and more candidate meadows (including the high-confidence ones), at the cost of more false positives.

Where the model works best

The model was trained on high-elevation meadows in the Sierra Nevada. The farther a watershed sits from that training area — lower elevations, or regions outside the Sierra — the less certain its predictions. Treat distant predictions as exploratory.
